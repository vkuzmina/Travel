**What’s your #1 tip for first timers visiting there?

*Don’t be intimidated by the high prices in Europe, it’s totally possible to travel on the cheap if you hitchhike and mix up your accommodation between couchsurfing and epic hostels. Remember – slow travel is cheap travel so bank lots of your time for your adventure and you’ll be sure to have an epic one! There’s many different ways to travel Europe and how ambitious you are should really depend on how much time you have – check out this helpful post for plenty of Europe backpacking itinerary suggestions.
